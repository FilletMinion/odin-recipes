Ice Soup Recipe

If you want to make some soup but you GENUINELY can't afford to, then this is for you!

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • Water

Directions

  1. Put water into a pot and bring to a boil.
  2. Grab some ice and grate some ice shavings into the water for flavor.
  3. Once the ice shavings dissolve, you can add in ice cubes.
  4. Stir for another minute then serve with some water sauce.
